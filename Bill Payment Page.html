Utility Bill Payments

Pay your utility bills conveniently at Sri Lanka Post locations or through our secure online platform

🏦 Available Services

Sri Lanka Post offers convenient bill payment services for various utilities and services. Pay your bills safely and securely at any of our locations island-wide.

Electricity Bills

  • Ceylon Electricity Board (CEB)
  • Lanka Electricity Company (LECO)
  • All provincial electricity boards

Water Bills

  • National Water Supply & Drainage Board
  • Provincial water boards
  • Local authority water supplies

Telecommunications

  • Sri Lanka Telecom (SLT)
  • Dialog Axiata PLC
  • Mobitel (Pvt) Ltd
  • Airtel Lanka

Government Services

  • EPF (Employees' Provident Fund)
  • ETF (Employees' Trust Fund)
  • Municipal council rates
  • Provincial council taxes

📋 How to Pay Your Bills

1

Choose Service

Select the type of bill you want to pay from the dropdown menu above.

2

Enter Details

Fill in your account number, amount, and personal details accurately.

3

Select Payment Method

Choose how you want to pay - cash, bank transfer, or online payment.

4

Visit Post Office

Visit your nearest Sri Lanka Post office with the payment details and required documents.

5

Get Receipt

Receive your official payment receipt as proof of transaction.

6

Keep Records

Keep your receipt safe for future reference and account verification.
